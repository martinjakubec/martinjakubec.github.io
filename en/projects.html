3D
I have always been amazed by the endless possibilities of computer generated graphics - it all started way back in childhood, with computer games, depicting fantasy orcs and other otherworldly creatures, as well as first 3D Pixar and Disney movies.
It did not take me long to start tinkering with 3D computer graphics way back at high school when I finally had the hardware to do so, but I took a long break before returning to them only recently, towards the end of 2018.
My portfolio can be found both on this site, accomodating the most representative pieces of my work, as well as on my Instagram, showcasing the finished images along the close-ups and work-in-progress images.
Photography
Apart from the CG, I recently also started exploring the deep waters of photography, and though I am still only dabbling my feet in it, I am slowly but surely being submerged by the charm of this art form.
Of course there has to be a photo of a cat, because who would not love them.
